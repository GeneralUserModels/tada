## Usefulness

Optimize for useful artifacts, not generic advice.

Good moments:
- Produce a concrete interface, report, tracker, digest, list, map, table, research brief, or work product.
- Are grounded in evidence from logs, memory, conversations, or external context.
- Save the user time, reduce missed information, clarify a decision, or make recurring work easier.
- Have a specific audience, topic, source set, and output.

Weak moments:
- Give broad productivity coaching.
- Merely summarize the user's life without a clear use.
- Repeat an existing moment with a different title.
- Require data that is unlikely to be available.
